Why I retrain in Web Development:

I have been working mainly as financial accountant in various sorts of organisations, SME for the first company and then in multinationals mainly. I have also worked as payroll accountant in French accounting practices
Through studying the English accounting qualification ACCA, early on I have tried to match my practical experience at the office with further enhancement of my knowledge of accounting
The work of a financial accountant has increasingly been influenced by the increasing extent of accounting and ERP systems, in the preparation of the financial reporting for instance. For the accountant profession, the deriving set of procedures has deeply evolved and developed. For the professionals, although this has meant increasing speed and lesser staff to achieve the same output as before, there has been a flip side, being the accounting activities being more complex and therefore abstract and this even more as the organisation is big and the scope of its activities wide. More than just carrying out the procedures and tasks, I have always tried to tackle the complexity of these systems and improve my understanding of them. How the accounting and economic events should be accounted and reported and how this translates with the tools and systems of big companies
The increasing use of database and the ever expanding capabilities of software applications in Finance, Accounting, Tax and Payroll have influenced my reflexion and increased my interest in acquiring coding skills and a deeper knowledge of web applications
Through my experience as payroll accountant, I have witnessed the increased expansion of web applications put in place by authorities to handle the reporting, return and payment of social contributions through internet
